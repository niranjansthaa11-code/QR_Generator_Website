<h1>QR Sathi </h1>
<hr>
This is a simple qr generator website .. you can generate qr for any website link or directly generate a whatsapp qr too !!
<hr>

<h3>How to use it ??</h3>
<ol>
  <li>type the text or url or whatsapp number</li>
  <li>click the show the magic or generate whatsapp qr</li>
  <li>you will get your qr !!</li>
  <li>if you wanna download click download and if you wanna close theres a option for that too :)</li>
</ol>
